How political is reddit?

Given the current nature of the world one might wonder how political different social media sites are

Reddit is one such site, and it is easy to quantify posts on this site due to multiple factors - very popular posts are put onto the front page of the site - posts have a numerical number to determine how many people like the post - since posts are from different subreddits multiple types of content gets put on the front page - posts on the front page are determined by how much people interact with them
